Tom Holland

Thomas Stanley Holland (born 1 June 1996) is an English actor. His accolades include a British Academy Film Award and three Saturn Awards. He featured on the Forbes 30 Under 30 Europe list of 2019. Some publications have called him one of the most popular actors of his generation.

Columbia-Pictures in association with Marvel Studios as production House, again started a Spider-Man movie franchise starring Tom Holland as Spider-Man. The first movie of this franchise got released in 2017 with the title "Spider-Man: Homecoming" and till now 2024, three movies has been releaed under this franchise, and few more projects are in pipeline.

The story line of Spider-Man: Homecoming was not at par with The Spider-Man 1 (2002) and The Amazing Spider-Man 1 (2012), but still it managed to score good on Box office and one the biggest reason was RDJ's supporting role in the movie. RDJ's name was enough to gether crowd in theaters. This movie also introduced one the badass Super-Villains i.e. Vulture. Unlike other villains of Spider-Man, the character of Vulture never appered in movies before this one. Till Homecoming Vulture appered only in comics and animated series "The Spectacular Spider-Man".
Till date this franchise has released 3 parts and these are:
1. Spider-Man: Homecoming.
2. Spider-Man: Far From Home.
3. Spider-Man: No Way Home.
Apart from these, few more parts are in progress. Tom Holland's Spider-Man was also introduces in Avengers franchise.

This franchise has introduced Super-Villains which never appeared in cinematic universe before and were only appeared in comic-verse, and these Super-Villains are:
1. Vulture - Played by Michael Keaton
2. Mysterio - Played by Jake Gyllenhaal
The 3rd part "No Way Home" introduces the true multiverse and all the 3 Spider-Man i.e. Tobey, Andrew and Tom come together including all the Super-Villains of all the parts. This part is one of the best Spider-Man movie because it brought the OG Spider-Man Tobey back again.
