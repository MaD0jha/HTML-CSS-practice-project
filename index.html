Who is Spider-Man !!

Spider-Man is super hero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. Spider-Man's character has been featured in comic books, television shows, films, video games, novels and plays.
Spider-Man's secret identity is Peter Benjamin Parker. Peter was an orphan raised by his Aunt May and Uncle Ben in New York City after his parents Richard and Mary Parker died in a plan crash. spider Man gets his super human spider-powers and abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. These powers include superhuman strength, agility, reflexes, stamina, durability, coordination, and balance; clinging to surfaces and ceilings like a spider; and detecting danger with his precognition ability called "Spider-Sense".
He builds wrist-mounted "web-shooter" devices that shoot artificial spider-webs of his own design, which he uses both for fighting and for web-swinging across the city.
Still, The Daily Bugle asks the biggest question "WHO IS SPIDER-MAN ? HERO or MENACE . . ."

The Super hero character Spider-Man is one of the most famous character in the world of comics and Marvel Cinematic Universe, especially amongs the teenagers. And the reason is teens find him very relatable, and relation is created because the problem Peter faces in his daily life is quite similar to a normal. No we are not talking about the problem of fighting with Super-Villains, let Spider-Man handle them, the problems we are talking about are, Peter also get late on his bills and rent just like us, he has to do odd jobs like pizza delivary to pay the college fee, he to get bullied in college just like some normal guy and finally, he to get nervous in front of his secret crush Marry Jane (aka M.J), quite relatable right ?

Actors who played the role

Different different production houses had made movies on the character of Spider-Man. The very first movie on Spider-Man was released in 2002 by Sony Pictures Entertainment. The movie was major hit and one the reason was the Actor who played Peter Parker i.e. Tobey Maguire. The supporting cast was also good, Kirsten Dunst played Mary Jane (M.J), Willem Dafoe played Green Goblin, James Franco played Harry Osborn (New Goblin) and ofcourse the classic "Stan Lee cameo". Sony pictures made a contract of 3 parts of Spider-Man movies with Tobey, the final part came in 2007.

In 2012, the character rights of Spider-Man were sold to Columbia Pictures. Columbia Pictures is also owned by Sony Pictures since 1989. This time the the role of Spider-Man was played by Andrew Garfield. In supporting role, Emma Stone played Gwen stacy, Rhys Owain played Dr. Curt Connors (Lizard Man), Irrfan Khan as Rajit Ratha. This franchise has only 2 parts.

In 2017, Columbia Pictures again made Spider-Man Movie named "Spider-Man: Homecoming" but this time Marvel Studios. was the production company. As Marvel Studios produced the movie therefore Spider-Man also got introduces with as Avenger. Tom Holland played Spider-Man this time, Zendaya Played M.J, Jacob Batalon played Ned (Peter's Friend) and last but not the least RDJ played Tony Stark. Tony recruits Peter as one of the team member of "The Avenger" (as Tony says 'The super Secret Boy Band. . . Duh').
