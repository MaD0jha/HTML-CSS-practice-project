Andrew Garfield

Andrew Russell Garfield (born 20 August 1983) is an English and American actor.various accolades, including a Tony Award, a BAFTA TV Award and a Golden Globe, in addition to nominations for a Primetime Emmy Award, a Laurence Olivier Award and two Academy Awards. Timeincluded Garfield on its list of 100 most influential people in the world in 2022.
